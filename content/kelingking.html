What Is Kelingking Beach ?

Kelingking Beach

The manta snorkeling Nusa Penida was the part of our Bali holiday we were the most excited about. A close encounter with these majestic creatures was definitely the highlight of our visit and now we share all the information you need to know about these giant creatures, how and where you can swim with them.

Manta Bay should in fact be called Manta Bays, because the site is a succession of bays surrounded by abrupt cliffs. Certain people would call the bay at the far east, Secret Manta. The bay is shallow, and the Manta are generally visible from the surface, which determines our entry point into the water.
